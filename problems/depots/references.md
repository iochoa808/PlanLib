## References

[FoxLong03]: PDDL2.1: An Extension to PDDL for Expressing Temporal Planning Domains
authors: Maria Fox, Derek Long
venue: Journal of Artificial Intelligence Research, 20:61–124, 2003
url: https://doi.org/10.1613/jair.1129
note: Describes IPC 2002 and its domains including Depots

[Bylander94]: The Computational Complexity of Propositional STRIPS Planning
authors: Tom Bylander
venue: Artificial Intelligence, 69(1–2):165–204, 1994
url: https://doi.org/10.1016/0004-3702(94)90081-7

[GereviniSerina02]: LPG: A Planner Based on Local Search for Planning Graphs with Action Costs
authors: Alfonso Gerevini, Ivan Serina
venue: Proceedings of AIPS-02 (6th International Conference on AI Planning and Scheduling), 2002
url: https://www.aaai.org/Library/AIPS/2002/aips02-010.php
note: LPG won the satisficing track of IPC 2002

[IPC02DepotsArchive]: IPC 2002 Depots Benchmark Instances
authors: IPC 2002 Organizers (Fox & Long)
venue: 3rd International Planning Competition (AIPS 2002), Toulouse, France, 2002
url: https://github.com/potassco/pddl-instances/tree/master/ipc-2002/domains/depots-strips-automatic
note: 22 STRIPS instances. Numeric, simple-time, and timed variants also available.
